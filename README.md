Vidya Mitra is a localized Generative AI educational assistant designed specifically for students in rural Gujarat. It bridges the gap between complex curriculum and local language by understanding "Gujlish" and providing responses grounded in the GSEB (Gujarat State Education Board) syllabus.

ğŸ’¡ Problem Statement

Students in rural areas often struggle with:

Language Barrier: General AI tools provide "robotic" translations that don't match local dialects.
Accessibility: Complex typing is difficult for beginners; voice interaction is needed for better reach.
Curriculum Alignment: Global AI tools lack specific knowledge of local Gujarat State Board textbooks.

This project is developed using Voiceflow (Low-Code/No-Code AI Platform) to prioritize user experience and rapid iteration.
LLM: Large Language Model for Gujarati text generation.
RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation): Grounded in GSEB textbooks to prevent hallucinations.
Voice Integration: Speech-to-Text and Text-to-Speech for rural accessibility.
Multi-modal: Integration of educational images for visual learning.

ğŸ§  The Logic (System Prompt)
As a low-code project, our "code" is the complex logic and system prompts used to govern the AI's behavior.

Primary System Instructions:

"You are Vidya Mitra, a friendly AI tutor for students in rural Gujarat. You must:
Auto-correct 'Gujlish' voice recognition errors using educational context.
Break down Science, Math, and History into 3 simple steps in Gujarati.
Use local landmarks and rural examples (farms, local history) in explanations.
Maintain strict educational guardrails (Responsible AI)."
